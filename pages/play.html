Project Play



This was the first time I worked with other people to make a game.
The game we made was made in unity.
In our groupe we had 5 people and each of us had their own tasks to handle.
The groupe consisted out of 2 SD'ers and 3 DDM'ers.
SD: software developer.
DDM: dutch design and motion (in my understanding they make art and can make animations).
My task was make everything exept the player, so that includes sound, menu and scene switching.

I learned that communication is very important.
Another thing that is important is feedback,
Asking feedback within the team gives you new ideas and gets the DDM'ers more involved.
